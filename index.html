Welcome to the ICT4M 2020

ICT4M 2020 is a conference that gathers academicians, researchers, professionals, and students to address the challenges of Information and Communication Technology (ICT) for the Muslim world. This conference has been conducted seven times so far with the most recent location in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia on 23-25 March 2020. The theme for this year is “Mental Health and Well Being in the 4th Industrial Revolution”.
